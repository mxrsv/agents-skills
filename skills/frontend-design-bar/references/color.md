# Color — accent as a system, surface as depth

- **One hot accent, used as a SYSTEM** (2-3 meaningful spots + consistent states),
  never random colored words. Reserve it → it fires on the **primary action** (CTA
  hover), the **focal artifact**, and **key data** (stat units, "live" dot).
- **Surface ramp for depth**, not a flat plane: `--surface-0…3` (dark) or
  `--paper-0…3` (light). Cards, dividers, glows differentiate layers.
- **Hairlines** at low alpha (`rgb(ink / 5–16%)`) for engineered structure; tighten
  corners (4–8px) for a precise feel, or big radii for a soft/glossy feel — pick one
  and commit (it's a vibe axis).
- **Background carries emotion**, not just a fill: bone-paper warmth, dark void,
  aurora mesh, blueprint field. Add a subtle **light source** (glow/bloom) tied to
  the accent — with restraint.
- Contrast check the accent on its surface (CTA fill: near-white text on vermillion
  reads; pale text on pale fails).
- Palette logic example (institutional/light): warm paper `#efe9dc → #fbf9f3`, warm
  ink `#16130d`, hairlines `rgb(22 19 13 / 5–16%)`, one vermillion `#e5421f`.
